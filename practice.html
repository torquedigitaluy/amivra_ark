This branch is the guided and listening-based support layer of the ecosystem — voice-led formats for reflection, alongside long-form environments built for rest and sleep.

Nothing here claims to fix or treat. These are quiet instruments: short enough for a busy day, gentle enough for the hour before sleep.

How this branch works

No app to install, no account beyond Insight Timer itself, no streak to maintain. Open a session when it's useful; close it when it's not.

3. Press play

4. Return when needed

No pressure to practice daily — only when it's useful.
