About

Who is MariAma

MariAma is a mindfulness practitioner, sound-based creator, and author. Her work brings together listening, guided practice, and writing in forms that are calm, usable, and grounded.

What she creates

One body of work, expressed in different forms.

Music

Ambient and reflective listening through Amivra Ark.

Practice

Guided meditations, sleep support, and long-form listening through Insight Timer.

Books

Reflective and practical written work published through Amazon.

Tools

Simple formats for clarity, resilience, and calmer everyday use.

Grounded background

A calm and practical foundation.

MariAma's work is rooted in mindfulness, reflective practice, and sound-based support. It is shaped by attention to what people can actually use in daily life and in supportive professional contexts.

Member of the Complementary Therapists Accreditation Association (UK)

Also active as an author, music creator, and guide for reflective audio formats.
